WHY SMOKE?

Smoke is a unique and differentiated approach to cooking the classic American burger. Smoked to perfection and then topped with ingredients and flavors from across the USA; our burger will take your guests on a gastronomic road trip. We delight our guest’s taste buds with award winning burgers like the Texas Triple Jay, Dirty Vegas and the Gilroy 5 Cheese and Garlic Burger!

One of the most important decisions we will make is selecting a Smoke the Burger Joint franchisee. When you join Smoke you become a part of the family and we want to make sure we are both a great fit for each other. Our franchisees are the face of Smoke the Burger Joint in their community and we work with you to deliver the best that our brand has to offer. We believe that our franchisees contribute to make us the best that we can be as well.

We want to make sure our you have the resources necessary to build out the Smoke the Burger Joint restaurant and execute on the brand promise.

We provide:

A Distinct Brand

Our unique concept is supported with innovative flavors, America’s favorite foods, a unique cooking process, design and branding that sets us apart from all others.

Site Selection Assistance

We use Buxton Co. Scout analytics to help identify areas that are home to our guests. We assist our franchisees in evaluating their site.

Vendor Partnerships

Smoke has the connections to assist in building out your restaurant. Design, construction, signage, furniture, fixtures, branding components. We are there to assist you along the way.

Support Services

Our Brand Resources Team is committed to assist you in delivering the brand promise to our guests. We work alongside our franchisees to build an exceptional guest experience.

Recipes

Our franchisee have access to an incredible selection of unique and flavorful menu offerings that are the key to Smoke’s success.

Systems

Our franchise program incorporates national food distribution, Aloha POS, the Ctuit Radar management tool, rewards program, mystery shop program, and operational best practices.

Marketing Support

Each location develops a community based marketing program. We provide supporting materials and tools to equip the franchisee to advertise and market locally.

Training

Our franchisees attend a minimum of four weeks training at one of our training locations and then receive an additional two weeks of on-site training once you open your store. Our franchise support team continues to provide assistance with regular visits to your location.

FAQ

Q: What is the total investment necessary to begin operations of a traditional Smoke franchise?

A: The total costs vary by location but our range is approximately $232,800 – $654,500 for a typical 2,500 square foot location.

Q: What kind of financial worth are you looking for in a candidate?

A: Each candidate should have non-leveraged personal resources of at least $150,000 and a net worth of $500,000.

Q: How much is the Franchise Fee and do you ever negotiate on the franchise fee?

A: The franchise fee for the first restaurant is $30,000. If a candidate is awarded additional stores the franchise fee is reduced to $25,000 for each additional store under the same ownership.

Q: Does Smoke offer financing?

A: No, we do not offer financing at this time.

Q: What is the average sales volume of a Smoke restaurant?

A: Due to Federal Trade Commission restrictions and various state regulations, we are not permitted to provide you with specific sales and profit projections. During the applications process you will receive a Franchise Disclosure Document that contains a list of current franchisees. You are welcome to call them to discuss their experiences.

Q: How much is the royalty fee and how is it collected?

A: The royalty fee is 5% of gross sales and is automatically withdrawn on a weekly basis.

Q: What is the Marketing/Production fund?

A: The Marketing/Production Fund is .5% of gross sales (half a percent) and is also collected on a weekly basis. The fund pays for the development of advertising, marketing materials and creative work.

Q: Is there an advertising/marketing fund?

A: Currently, there is not a national advertising fund although the agreement allows for an advertising fund to be established of up to 2% of gross sales. Today, the franchisee is required to spend a minimum of 2% of gross sales on local store advertising. This expenditure is not paid to the franchisor but an accounting of the expenditure is submitted quarterly.

Q: What is the term for the franchise?

A: The initial term is for 10 years, with a 10 year option.

Q: Is there a renewal fee after the initial term?

A: Yes, after the initial term, the renewal fee is $15,000 or 50% of the then current franchise fee, whichever is greater.

Q: How long does it take to open my restaurant?

A: There are a lot of factors that determine the length of time to open, such as site selection, lease negotiations, construction, etc. You have eight months from the signing of the franchise agreement to open the restaurant.

Q: What types training is offered?

A: Each franchisee starts their training by spending a minimum of four weeks working in an approved Smoke training site. Various members of our training team will spend an additional two weeks in the franchisee’s restaurant at the time of opening.
