The place for Handcrafted Burgers and Gourmet Grilled Cheese.

Over 130 years after the hamburger was invented, two award-winning pitmasters found a better way to cook them! Fresh Angus beef, low-smoked over hickory wood, gives the patties at Smoke the Burger Joint a particularly flavorful sense of “umami.”

Raving fans line-up early for handcrafted burgers inspired by flavor combinations from unique regions across the country. The Texas Triple Jay, Dirty Vegas, Gilroy 5 Cheese Garlic, Virginia Beach and San Jose Bánh Mi have all won first place awards at the prestigious National Hamburger Festival.

Discover your favorite Road Trip burger with a side of fresh-cut fries or housemade onion rings and wash it down with a craft beer (select locations) or a hand-spun milkshake. Gourmet grilled cheese or smokehouse dogs and wings are proving to be just as popular as the burgers.

Try all that Smoke has to offer. Your taste buds will thank you!
